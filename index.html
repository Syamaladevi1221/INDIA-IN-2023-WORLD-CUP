Ten national teams participated in the tournament, which was hosted by India. It started on 5 October and concluded on 19 November 2023

    Sachin Tendulkar

     Sachin Tendulkar is a retired Indian international cricketer who is widely regarded as one of the
     greatest  batsmen in the history of cricket. He was born on April 24, 1973, in Mumbai, India. Tendulkar
     is often referred to as the "Little Master" and the "God of Cricket" by his fans.
     
     Cricket World Cup: Tendulkar played in six Cricket World Cups for India, helping the team win the
     tournament in 2011. He was part of the Indian team that reached the finals in 2003 as well.
     
     Cricket Achievements: Sachin achieved several milestones during his career, including being the first
      player to reach 10,000 runs in ODIs and the first to score a double century in ODIs.
     
     "Sachin Tendulkar" is not only celebrated for his exceptional cricketing skills but also for his
     sportsmanship, humility, and contributions to the growth of cricket in India and globally.

    MS Dhoni

     Mahendra Singh Dhoni is an Indian professional cricketer, who plays as a wicket-keeper
     batsman. Widely regarded as one of the world's greatest wicket-keeper batsmen and captains
     in the history of the sport, he is known for his explosive batting, wicket-keeping and
     leadership skills.
     
     'Dhoni' was widely regarded as one of the best finishers in limited-overs cricket. His
     ability to stay calm under pressure and finish matches with his aggressive yet calculated
     batting style earned him the nickname "Captain Cool."

    Virat Kohli

     
     
     'Virat Kohli' is an Indian international cricketer and the former captain of the Indian national cricket
     team. He currently represents Royal Challengers Bangalore in the IPL and Delhi in domestic cricket.
     Kohli is widely regarded as one of the greatest batsmen in the history of the sport, and the best of this
     era. He is the highest run scorer in T20I and IPL.
     
     Batting Records: Kohli is renowned for his exceptional batting skills and consistency across all formats
     of the game. He has set numerous records, including being one of the fastest players to reach 8,000,
     9,000, 10,000, and 11,000 runs in One Day Internationals.
     
     There are many other nicknames given to him by fans as well. However, 'Captain Fearless' and 'King Kohli'
     are the two popular names for Virat.
     
     Kohli made his international debut for the Indian cricket team in August 2008 in an One Day International
     (ODI) against Sri Lanka. He later made his Test debut in 2011 against West Indies.

    Rohit Sharma

     
     
     'Rohit Gurunath Sharma' is an Indian international cricketer and the current captain of India national
     cricket team in all formats. Considered one of the best batsmen of his generation and one of greatest
     opening batters of all time, Sharma is known for his timing, elegance, six-hitting abilities and
     leadership skills.
     
     Batting Style: Rohit is known for his stylish and aggressive batting. He is particularly effective as an
     opening batsman in limited-overs cricket.
     
     Rohit Sharma Cricket Academy: Apart from his playing career, Rohit Sharma has been actively involved in
     promoting and nurturing young talents through the Rohit Sharma Cricket Academy.
     
     Rohit is called as "Hitman".
